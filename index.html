IMPROMPTU READING

By Reet Chohan in Books

Hi my name is Reet Chohan. I go through reading slumps and blocks from time to time. Although I prefer dystopian novels, I will read anything that interests me. This website was developed to keep track of the books I read and to write book reviews.
